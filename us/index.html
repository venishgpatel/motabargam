Welcome to
The Mota Bar Gam Kadva Patidar Samaj
USA
This is a social non-profit group of families from Mota Bar Gam Kadva Patidar Samaj living in the United States. The purpose of this website is for the people of Samaj to be able to find and share information about Samaj families, stay connected and help each other.
